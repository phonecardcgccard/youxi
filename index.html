Memory Training Tips

  • Practice regularly – even 10 minutes daily makes a difference.
  • Gradually increase difficulty to keep challenging your brain.
  • Focus on accuracy first, then work on speed.
  • Combine memory training with physical exercise for best results.
  • Ensure adequate sleep to consolidate improvements.
